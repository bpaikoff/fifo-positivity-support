About FIFO Positivity

FIFO Positivity is a mindfulness and gratitude app that helps you maintain focus on your most recent positive experiences. Using a simple First-In-First-Out (FIFO) queue system, the app keeps your 10 most recent positive moments active while automatically archiving older ones.

Key Features

  • Keep 10 active positive moments in your daily practice
  • Automatic FIFO rotation when you add new experiences
  • 5 customizable daily reminder notifications
  • Searchable archive of all your moments
  • 100% private - all data stored locally on your device
  • Beautiful, calming design with dark mode support

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the FIFO queue work?

Your app maintains 10 active experiences. When you add an 11th experience, the oldest one automatically moves to your archive. This keeps your focus on recent positive moments while preserving everything you've added.

Can I customize notification times?

Yes! Go to the Settings tab and tap on any of the 5 daily reminder times to customize them to fit your schedule.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your experiences are stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. No accounts required, no cloud sync, no tracking.

What happens to archived experiences?

When an experience is archived, it's preserved forever in your Archive tab. You can search through all your archived moments and view them organized by month.

Can I manually delete experiences?

Yes, you can delete any active experience or archived experience at any time. You can also clear your entire archive if needed.

How do I disable notifications?

You can disable notifications in your iPhone Settings → Notifications → FIFO Positivity. Or simply turn off specific reminder times in the app's Settings tab.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection: We do not collect any personal data. All information you enter in FIFO Positivity stays on your device.

Third-Party Services: We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Notifications: Local notifications are generated on your device and do not transmit any data.

Your Rights: Since all data is stored locally, you have complete control. Delete the app to remove all data.
